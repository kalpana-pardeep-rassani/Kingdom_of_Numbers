The Idea

The challenge

  • Math practice can feel repetitive.
  • Students often do not get a learning path that fits them.
  • Progress is not always easy to see.

The response

  • Turn practice into short game missions.
  • Use story, feedback, and progress tracking.
  • Keep the math clear and syllabus-based.
